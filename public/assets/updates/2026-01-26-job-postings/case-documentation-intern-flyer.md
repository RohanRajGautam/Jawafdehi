# Case Documentation Intern

## About Jawafdehi

**Building Nepal's Institutional Memory Against Corruption**

At _jawafdehi.org_, we're creating an open database of corruption cases for Nepal, making allegations, evidence, and outcomes accessible to citizens. Born out of the GenZ mandate to end the culture of corruption in Nepal, Jawafdehi will remain a civic tech accountability platform, directly challenging [CIAA](ciaa.gov.np) and the rest of Nepal's public accountability system.

Join us in documenting corruption and strengthening accountability through rigorous research and structured data.

See example cases:
- Rabi Lamichhane cooperative fraud: https://jawafdehi.org/case/207
- Giri Bandhu land swap scandal: https://jawafdehi.org/case/175

1. **Jawafdehi.org**: The primary platform. [Learn more here.](https://docs.google.com/document/d/11ZF3_kXLbvzyjXKn1F1x17wj0wyzypjJg7XMijifRVA/)

## Key Responsibilities

### Case Research & Documentation
- **Investigate corruption allegations** from news sources, court documents, and public records
- **Verify and cross-reference sources** to ensure accuracy and credibility
- **Structure case information** including timelines, entities, evidence, and outcomes
- **Extract data from documents** using OCR and manual review
- **Conduct diligent case research** following established methodologies

### Data & Research
- **Nepal Government research** - Track government entities, officials, and organizational structures
- **Document evidence** - Organize and categorize supporting materials
- **Create case timelines** - Map events chronologically with supporting evidence
- **Quality assurance** - Review and validate case data for completeness

### Collaboration
- **Work with the development team** to improve case management tools
- **Contribute to research methodologies** and documentation standards
- **Participate in case review** discussions

### Media and Outreach
- **Create scripts and other materials** for sharing corruption cases in the media.
